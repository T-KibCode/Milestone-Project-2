Deadly Combat!

A 2 Player Couch Fighting Game from a bygone era! A Strictly 2-player 2d fighting game for you and a friend! Enter Your name below and begin the battle to see who will be the victor!

Player 1

  • Attack = Spacebar
  • Move Left = A
  • Move Right = D
  • Jump = W

Player 2

  • Attack = Arrow Down
  • Move Left = Arrow Left
  • Move Right = Arrow Right
  • Jump = Arrow Up

*Please note: you do not have to enter your name to play if you would prefer to be anonymous! Just refresh the page and start the game over!*
